Instructions

  • Enter minimum/maximum values for your armor sets in the empty boxes on the right.
  • Choose which armor pieces to include by selecting them in the "allowed x armor" windows.
  • Press "Run" to see a list of armor sets using your criteria.
  • Remove an armor piece from your allowed list by clicking the [x] button to the right of the result
  • Save an armor set you like by clicking the empty box [ ] to the left of the results.
  • Press "Reset" to go back to the original settings.
